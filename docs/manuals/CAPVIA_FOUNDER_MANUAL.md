# CAPVIA Founder Manual

This document is a founder-level manual detailing product value propositions, monetization strategies, sales scripts, demo workflows, pilot programs, and product expansion roadmaps.

---

## 1. Product Value Proposition

CAPVIA solves the core problem of tech recruiting for startups and enterprises: **high resume screening volume combined with cheating on remote technical assessments.**

Traditional recruiting funnels look like this:
1. **Resume Screening**: Sift through thousands of resumes manually or via basic keyword-based ATS tools.
2. **Technical Assessment**: Candidates take non-proctored coding challenges. Cheating via ChatGPT, copilot, or external help is common, rendering scores unreliable.
3. **Interviews**: Spend hours in live interviews, only to realize the candidate's skills do not match their assessment score.

### The CAPVIA Solution
CAPVIA automates the entire funnel with built-in security and deep analytics:
- **Semantic Resume Screening**: Matches skills and experiences semantically using embeddings, filtering out noise.
- **Cheating-Resistant Coding**: Captures copy-paste events, tab switches, and code creation speeds.
- **Proctored Video Interviews**: Video Q&A with facial focus and visibility tracking.
- **Consolidated Analytics**: Combines technical skills, communication, and proctoring metrics into a **Weighted Leaderboard Score** and a **9-Dimensional DNA Radar Profile**.

## 3. Product Pitch & Demo Script

Use this workflow to demo CAPVIA to prospective clients (recruiters, heads of engineering, and HR leaders):

### Step 1: The Bottleneck
*“Think about your last backend engineer listing. You probably received over 800 applications in 48 hours. How many of those did you screen manually? And how many candidates who scored highly on coding assessments failed your live interview because they used AI to write their code?”*

### Step 2: The Stepper
*“We built CAPVIA to solve this. When a candidate applies, they enter our automated pipeline. They are matched semantically, invited to our proctored coding challenge, and transition directly into a video interview.”*

### Step 3: Recruiter Leaderboard Demo
*“Look at this leaderboard. You don’t just see a coding score. You see their composite rank, their Trust Index, and their DNA Radar Profile. Our Integrity Engine calculated that this applicant had a 98% focus score with zero tab switches, whereas another candidate’s score was flagged as high-risk due to multiple tab switches and copy-paste events.”*

### Step 4: The Report Dossier
*“With one click, you generate a recruiter dossier. This PDF outlines their strengths, weaknesses, and a structured hiring recommendation. You can hand this directly to your hiring manager and skip hours of manual validation.”*

## 4. Startup Pilot Program Playbook

To onboard startups and validate the platform, run **4-week pilot programs**:
1. **Week 1 (Onboarding)**: Set up the client's corporate workspace, verify recruiters, and configure custom weights for their active job openings.
2. **Week 2 (Candidate Funneling)**: Route all applicants through the CAPVIA Candidate Portal.
3. **Week 3 (Leaderboard Analysis)**: Conduct a checkpoint review with the HR team to analyze candidates in the Gold and Platinum recommendation tiers.
4. **Week 4 (Conversion)**: Compare the candidates hired through CAPVIA against traditional sourcing channels to demonstrate time-to-hire savings.
